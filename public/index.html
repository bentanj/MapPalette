How MapPalette Works

1
Create Your Account
Sign up for MapPalette in just a few clicks. Join our community of creative runners!
Simply provide your email and create a password. No credit card required. Start exploring our features immediately after verification.
2
Design Your Route
Use our intuitive map interface to draw your running route in creative shapes and patterns.
Click points on the map to create your route. Our smart algorithm helps you follow roads and paths automatically.
3
Customize Your Experience
Add personal touches to your route with custom markers, descriptions, and difficulty ratings.
Mark interesting spots, add notes about terrain, and set privacy settings for your routes.
4
Share and Connect
Share your creative routes with friends or discover routes created by other runners.
Connect with fellow runners, join challenges, and get inspired by the community's creative routes.
