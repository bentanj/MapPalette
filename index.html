About Us

MapPalette is developed by RunGoWhere Pte Ltd, a passionate team from SMU. Our app allows you to draw creative routes on maps while exercising, making fitness more fun and purposeful. Whether you're a casual runner or looking to spice up your workouts, MapPalette offers a unique way to stay fit and express your creativity.
